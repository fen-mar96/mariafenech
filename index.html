Who am I?

Maria Fenech is a young visual artist with a strong passion for photography. Maria is seeking to discover the wonders of the world through her art and photography. She finds herself captivated by the splendour of architecture and geometry, and also by the delicate beauty of the human anatomy. The fondness Maria has for these subjects, and her knowledge of them, greatly influence her photography and artwork.

Maria has a Diploma in Design Foundation Studies and is currently reading for her Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) Honours in Digital Arts at the University of Malta. Her drive and determination always push her to experiment and learn more with each passing day.
